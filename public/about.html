About Me

I am a dedicated IT professional with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration (GIMPA), where I graduated in 2025. With over a year of experience in networking, programming, and technical support, I specialize in database management, network security protocols, and Cisco technologies. My technical skills include proficiency in Python, Java, SQL, and web development, complemented by hands-on experience in data analysis and machine learning projects.

During my time at GIMPA, I served as the Financial Secretary for the GIMPA Association of Technology Students (GATS) from 2024 to 2025 and as a Class Representative from 2023 to 2025, demonstrating my leadership and teamwork abilities. I am passionate about leveraging my analytical skills and problem-solving expertise to drive innovative solutions in data science and IT. My adaptability, effective communication, and commitment to continuous learning enable me to thrive in dynamic, collaborative environments.
